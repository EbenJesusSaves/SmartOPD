Our Services

Online medical consultations

It has never been simpler or more economical to consult a doctor. When you contact one of our trained and certified medical doctors, you will receive individualized, first-rate care. Our call center is available around-the-clock, and you can communicate with us via phone, video, and chat in English and a number of regional languages.

Laboratory

Since we are aware that not everyone feels well enough to leave their homes, our skilled laboratory professionals are prepared to visit you whenever you require their services. After our Medical Doctors have given you a thorough explanation, the results are shared with you electronically.

Pharmacy

Deliveries of prescription drugs and other pharmacy orders can be made quickly. For patients with chronic conditions who require a recurring supply of their medication, we also provide prescriptions.

Clinic

Every time you visit the SMARTOPD Clinic, spend less time waiting and get top-notch care.

Vaccination Services

Call us to set up an appointment if you, your kid, or anybody else living in your home requires a vaccination. In the convenience of your home or at the clinic, our knowledgeable nurses administer vaccinations.

Annual Healthcare Plans

We are aware that you are searching for trustworthy, convenient healthcare. Get unlimited medical consultations all year long when you purchase one of our subscription options.
